New Motherhood

AWARENESS

1.) Black mothers are 4 times more likely to experience PTSD and less likely to seek or receive mental health treatment.

2.) Black mothers needs for paid parental leave are mostly unmet, which causes economic problems due to unemployment or lost wages during leave.

CARE STEPS

1.) Make post-giving birth care plan with health provider and have contact within 3 weeks of giving birth.

2.) Get ongoing medical care as needed and do a complete chechup no longer than 12 weeks after giving birth.

3.) Advocate for yourself and speak out if there is something that feels wrong with yourself or your baby.

4.) Join a new mother support group or have a meeting with therapists to ensure healthy mental state.

5.) For post vaginal birth, you can do light excercises, including Kegel excercises to help strengthen pelvic muscles.

6.) For post cesarean birth, don't lift from squatting position or lift anything heavier than the baby.

7.) Surround yourself with supportive people, family, and friends. Reach out if you need help!
